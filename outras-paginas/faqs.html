Square has a number of plans available. The Free and Pro Plans are ideal for individual users looking to schedule content for their personal social accounts. The Business Plans are ideal for teams and agencies looking to schedule content for their business or client social accounts, with the ability to invite team members to collaborate, and have access to advanced analytics.

The deciding factors tend to be:

  • - The number of social accounts you're looking to schedule content to
  • - The number of team members who will need access to Buffer Publish
  • - The number of posts you're looking to schedule in advance
  • - Whether or not you require in-depth analytics
